Welcome to the classic minesweeper game. 
Your goal is to open all the cells on the field
But each cell can contain a mine, and if a cell
with a mine is opened, game is over. 


game rules:
Size of a field is 8x8 
chances of a mine for each
cell is 16%
first move is always safe

game rules can be changed by adjusting custom
values in the following source file. Alter
constant variables FIELD_SIZE and
CHANCES_OF_MINE to your wish


when program launched, you will be asked for
coordinates of your first turn. Enter two
numbers from 1-8. Then, a field
will be outputted. Once again, you will be
asked for coordinates of your next turn. 
Enter your coordinates. To put a
flag, indicating your guesses on possible mine
location, input "0" for your first number. 
After doing so, input your coordinates for
your flag.


Legend:
1-8 number of mines in adjacent cells
- cell with no mines in adjacent cells
* mine
? unopened cell


thanks for playing, good luck!
